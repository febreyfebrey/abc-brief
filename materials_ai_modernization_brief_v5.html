01 Why now為什麼現在做 Context背景

Our teaching materials are visually and editorially aging. Some AI Personalization content dates back to 1998. Manual rework cannot scale to 47K materials — we need an AI batch approach that keeps teaching quality high and moves fast. 我們的教材在視覺與內容上都已老舊，部分 AI Personalization 教材最早可追溯到 1998 年。47K 份教材無法靠人工逐份翻新——需要 AI 批次處理，在維持教學品質的同時快速執行。

How old it is, and whether it mentions outdated facts. 教材年份、是否引用過時內容。
Quality品質
Student review, completion rate, teacher feedback. 學生評分、完課率、老師回饋。

05 Three phases, gated by results 三階段執行，每階段都有檢核點 Roadmap

Each phase has clear go/no-go criteria. We commit to the next phase only when the previous one passes. 每個階段都有明確的 go/no-go 標準。前一階段通過，才進下一階段。

Phase 01
Proof of conceptPOC 概念驗證
6 weeks · 1 product6 週 · 1 個產品
Oxford Speak Now (128 materials). Recent, clean, small enough to manage. The goal isn't to fix Oxford Speak Now itself — the goal is to build and prove the pipeline we'll use everywhere. Oxford Speak Now（128 份）。產品新、結構乾淨、規模可控。目的不是修好 Oxford Speak Now 本身——目的是建立並驗證未來會用在所有產品線的 pipeline。
Phase 02
Rolling validation滾動驗證
Two parallel tracks兩條 Track 並行
Once POC passes, we extend in two directions at once. POC 通過後，雙線同時擴展。
Track A Roll visual refresh out to other product lines (Cambridge, NatGeo, Oxford Discover, Cambridge Prepare). 純視覺翻新擴展到其他產品線（Cambridge、NatGeo、Oxford Discover、Cambridge Prepare）。
Track B Pilot visual + content on AI Personalization, starting with the Do group. AI Personalization 試做視覺＋內容優化，從 Do 組開始。
Phase 03
Full rollout全量 rollout
Both tracks at scale雙 Track 規模化
Process the full Do group in batches. Run scheduled refreshes for Defer. Retire the Deprecate set. Do 組全量批次處理。Defer 組排程進行翻新。Deprecate 組下架。

08 Risks & open questions風險與待解問題 Tracking追蹤中

Risks we know about. None is a blocker; each has a plan in motion. 我們已知的風險。沒有任何一項是 blocker；每項都有對應計畫。

Licensing on branded content品牌內容授權
Oxford, Cambridge and NatGeo have brand guidelines for how their content is presented. May need approval.Oxford、Cambridge、NatGeo 對教材呈現有品牌規範，可能需要授權確認。
Action. Legal & BD review in parallel with POC. We assume approval for POC scope, and confirm before scaling.法務與 BD 在 POC 階段並行確認。POC 範圍假設可行，擴大規模前先取得確認。
Teacher adaptation老師適應
New visuals mean teachers need to re-learn. A bad launch creates classroom problems.新視覺需要老師重新熟悉。Rollout 沒做好會在課堂上出問題。
Action. Phased launch per product line. Teacher preview window with notes.按產品線分階段 rollout。設老師預覽期，並附上更新說明。
Content quality on Track BTrack B 內容品質
AI-assisted content edits risk subtle errors or shifts in difficulty.AI 輔助內容編輯有風險，可能產生細微錯誤或難度偏移。
Action. AI auto-handles only safe edits like typos. Anything beyond that requires content editor review. Every batch is sampled before release.AI 只自動處理安全修補（如 typo）。其他變動都需要內容編輯審稿。每批上線前都會抽樣檢查。
Editing after launch上線後的編輯
Content team will want to edit materials after launch. Re-running the full pipeline for small fixes is wasteful.內容團隊上線後會想編輯教材。為了小修改重跑整條 pipeline 太浪費。
Action. A simple HTML editor delivered with Phase 02. Scope finalized after POC.Phase 02 同步交付一個簡易 HTML 編輯器。POC 結束後確認 scope。
